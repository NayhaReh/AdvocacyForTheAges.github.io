Who We Are

AFA is a student-led advocacy organization at the University of Calgary, dedicated to promoting the wellbeing, dignity, and safety of both children and elderly populations. Our team consists of undergraduate students from diverse academic backgrounds, united by a commitment to intergenerational advocacy, education, and community engagement.

We work to bridge gaps between age groups by raising awareness, supporting advocacy initiatives, and strengthening connections among students, faculty, and community organizations. We believe that meaningful change begins with education, collaboration, and action.

By partnering with campus resources, advocacy centres, and local organizations, we amplify existing efforts while creating opportunities for students to contribute to real-world impact. Through outreach initiatives, educational programming, and advocacy campaigns, AFA strives to foster informed communities and empower individuals to engage with issues that affect quality of life across generations

Our Mission

AFA is dedicated to advancing advocacy, education, and community connection across generations. We work to improve the quality of life, safety, and dignity of both young and elderly populations through awareness, collaboration, and student-led action.
